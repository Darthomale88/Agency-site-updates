About

Built for the small business owner.

Thirteen years in IT, a hand-coded approach, and direct access to the person actually building your site.

I'm Darryl, and I build websites for small businesses in Indianapolis. By day, I work in IT for Indiana state government, the kind of job where uptime and reliability aren't optional. Before that, I supported clinicians and administrators in healthcare IT. Thirteen years of keeping critical systems running for people who couldn't afford for them to fail. That's the same standard I bring to your website.

I'm not an agency. It's just me, which means no account managers, no outsourced code, and no markup for overhead I don't have. You'll talk to the person actually building your site, from your first conversation to the day it launches. I got into this during COVID, rekindling a creative side I'd had since college, and taught myself the rest. No templates, no page builders. I architect every site myself and use AI to help write the code faster, the same way a contractor uses power tools instead of hand saws. The skill is in knowing what to build, not how slowly I can type.

I'm also a husband and father of four, soon to be five, here in Indianapolis. This isn't a side hustle I'll abandon when it gets hard. It's something I'm building carefully, the same way I'd want someone building something for my family.
