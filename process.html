How It Works

From idea to live website.

From first conversation to launch day — here's exactly what to expect when you work with YourWebBuild.

01

Free Consultation

We start with a focused consultation to learn about your business, goals, and vision.

02

Proposal & Agreement

You receive a clear project proposal with pricing and timeline. Once approved, we sign an agreement and collect your deposit to lock in your start date.

03

Content Collection

We send you a simple checklist — logo, photos, business info, and copy. Timeline begins once your materials are received.

04

Design & Build

We get to work. Your first draft is typically ready within 5–7 business days. You'll get a preview link to review before anything goes live.

05

Revisions

You share feedback and we refine until it's right. Included revisions are outlined in your proposal — no surprise charges.

06

Launch

Final payment clears, we connect your domain, and your site goes live. We walk you through everything so you feel confident owning it.
